Welcome to Ouchi Gohan

In Japanese, “Ouchi Gohan” means “home-cooked meal.” At Ouchi Gohan, we believe that even the simplest dishes can bring warmth, joy, and connection to the table. You don’t need to be an expert chef or have access to rare ingredients to enjoy Japanese meals. What truly matters is the feeling behind the food—cooking slowly, sharing joy with loved ones, and finding comfort in each bite.

Whether you are a student, a busy parent, or just someone who wants to make good meals at home, our recipes are designed to be simple and comforting. Many of them use ingredients you can find at local grocery stores, and most dishes can be prepared in under 30 minutes.

Explore easy recipes, learn simple techniques, and enjoy stories connected to everyday Japanese cooking. Whether you’re far from Japan or discovering these dishes for the first time, this space will help you bring honest, heartfelt flavors into your kitchen.
